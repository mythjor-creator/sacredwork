{# ── What we're building ── #}

A marketplace built for your work.

Most booking platforms were designed for gyms and hair salons. The intake forms, the categories, how services get described — none of it was built with you in mind.

Clairbook is different. We're building a space for practitioners whose work requires real trust, nuance, and a client who gets what they're booking. That means better discovery, better context, and a booking experience that honors the quality of your practice.
