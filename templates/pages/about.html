About Clairbook

A marketplace for practitioners who work in many languages.

"Clair" comes from the clairs: clairvoyant, clairaudient, clairsentient, and beyond. Clairbook honors intuitive traditions while welcoming grounded, practical approaches. We believe people should be able to find support that fits their worldview without being forced into someone else's categories.

Who belongs here

Practitioners across sacred, holistic, and practical disciplines are welcome. If your work helps people heal, grow, regulate, process, integrate, or reconnect, Clairbook is built for you.

  • Sacred beauty
  • Holistic wellness
  • Tarot readers
  • Astrologers
  • Lightworkers
  • Somatic practitioners
  • Integrative coaches
  • Practical mindset support

No one needs to frame their practice as overtly spiritual to be included. Clairbook is intentionally plural: mystical, practical, evidence-informed, and intuitive work can coexist here with clarity and respect.

What makes Clairbook different

  • Profiles are designed for nuance, not just labels.
  • Service pages communicate delivery format, context, and pricing clearly.
  • Clients can discover fit through values and approach, not only by category.
  • Practitioners can publish in their own voice.
