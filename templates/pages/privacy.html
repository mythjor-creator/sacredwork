Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

1. Introduction

SacredWork ("we," "us," or "our") operates the clairbook platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone, business name, practice type, modalities, location
  • Profile Information: Bio, services offered, pricing, availability, credentials
  • Communications: Your messages to us, support requests, feedback
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, searches performed, events tracked (anonymously for analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the platform
  • To send you service updates, waitlist status changes, and notifications (only as you've requested)
  • To provide customer support
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To prevent fraud and abuse

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information:

  • With service providers who assist our operations (hosting, email delivery)
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete all personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

  • Access: Request a copy of your data at /account/export-data/
  • Delete: Request account deletion at /account/delete/
  • Correct: Update your profile information anytime through your account
  • Opt-out: Email us at privacy@clairbook.app to disable notifications

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS, secure cookies) to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Contact Us

For privacy questions or data requests, email: privacy@clairbook.app
